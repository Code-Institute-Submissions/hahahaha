Bio

The man, the myth, the legend. Tiago was born in the Portuguese island of Madeira (Portugal). He moved to Ireland aged 5 and grew up in a very musical family. A multi-instrumentalist who has worked with so many bands, he has recently resurfaced some of his old demos and begun to restore them by today's standards. An up and coming talent and labeled by some as a genius, Tiago mostly performs and writes music when he is not writing code in his day job. With a deep interest in hard rock, Tiago vows to bring back to restore his beloved genre back to life.
